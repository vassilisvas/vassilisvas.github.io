--- layout: page title: About Me subtitle: Research Associate - RISE, Cyprus ---

My research interests lie in the area of bio-inspired artificial intelligence, focusing on reinforcement learning, neural networks and evolutionary computation. I obtained a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Cyprus in 2007, an M.Sc. in Intelligent Systems Engineering from the University of Birmingham, U.K., in 2008, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Cyprus in 2015. I worked as a post-doctoral researcher and research engineer at Inria Nancy - Grand-Est, France, team LARSEN, from 2015 to 2018, on the ERC-funded ResiBots project, which aims to develop algorithms for low-cost robots to autonomously recover from unforeseen damages in a matter of minutes. Currently, I am an associate research fellow at the University of Cyprus and a research associate at Research Centre on Interactive Media, Smart Systems and Emerging Technologies, Cyprus, under the Socially-Competent Robotic and Agent Technologies (SoCRATes) Multidisciplinary Research Group.
